Milk Caramel

A heavenly delight that melts in your mouth

How about that?

Milk caramel, also known as dulce de leche, is a beloved confection made by slowly heating sweetened milk until it transforms into a rich, creamy caramel spread.

Interesting Facts

Origin Story

Milk caramel originated in Latin America, with Argentina and Uruguay both claiming to be its birthplace.

Making Process

Traditional preparation involves heating milk and sugar for several hours until the mixture caramelizes.

Global Appeal

This treat is enjoyed worldwide under different names: doce de leite (Brazil), manjar (Chile), and arequipe (Colombia).
